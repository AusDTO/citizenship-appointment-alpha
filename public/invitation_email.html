Important notes about booking your citizenship appointment

  • Please select the soonest available appointment that suits you.
  • Appointments run for approximately 2 hours.
  • You are required to schedule an appointment within 2 weeks of receiving this information.
  • You can schedule your appointment a maximum of 3 times.
  • You can reschedule your appointment up to 24hrs prior to your appointment. If you do not attend 2 appointments in total, a decision may be made on your application.

You must bring the following documents with you

Evidence of when you first arrived in Australia and your present country of citizenship

Current and previous passport, or
Travel document with visa
E.g. Document for Travel to Australia (DFTTA), PLO56 (M56), Titre de Voyage

Evidence of your identity

A document with your photo and signature
E.g. Australian driver licence, National identity card or current passport
Proof of your current residential address
E.g. Utilities or bank notice, rental contract or rates notice

Evidence of your date of birth, birth name and any changes of name

Birth certificate
Marriage or divorce certificate, if applicable
Australian Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages as evidence of other changes of name, if applicable

Evidence of good character (if applicable)

Overseas penal clearance
Determine whether you need to provide this document via www.border.gov.au/Trav/Visa/Char

Evidence of your identity in the community (if applied online)

Form 1195 - Identity declaration
Bring the original form which you completed and attached to your online application.

Documents for children

The following documents are required for all children under 16 years of age

A full birth certificate or family register containing parent’s details
A Passport or travel document used to enter Australia (if not on your passport)
Evidence of any name change between birth and current name, if applicable
If you have been granted custody and/or guardianship of the child, bring the relevant court orders.
If the child is adopted, bring the adoption papers.
