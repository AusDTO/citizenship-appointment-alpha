What will happen at your appointment

  • You will be at the Citizenship centre for up to 2 hours in total.
  • We will check the original documents you bring to your appointment against the information and copies of the documents you provided in your application, for all applicants including children.
  • We will also take your photograph and collect your signature. This will help us to verify your identity at your appointment, confirm whether your application is valid, and whether you are eligible to sit the citizenship test at this appointment.
  • You will then have 45 minutes to complete the test which consists of 20 multiple choice questions.

If you have included children in your application they are not required to attend this appointment. There are no childcare facilities so you will need to make alternative childcare arrangements for your children.
